FAQ

Questions, answered.

How do you verify I'm from IIT or IIM?

During signup, you provide your LinkedIn profile URL. Our team manually reviews your education section and approves only verified IIT or IIM alumni and students. You'll receive an email once a decision is made, typically within a few hours.

Is automating LinkedIn safe for my account?

We throttle activity well below LinkedIn's usage patterns, humanize pauses, and never scrape. All actions are authorized by you via OAuth.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes. Pause anytime from Settings → Billing. Refunds on longer plans are pro-rated.

What happens to my data?

Your resume, preferences, and messages are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and transit. You can delete everything with one click.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026
Quick Summary: Your privacy is our highest priority. We collect only the data needed to perform automated applications on your behalf. We never sell your data, and you can delete it at any time.

1. Data We Collect

To run your Job Seeker Agent, we collect and process the following information:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, password hash, and institution verification info.
  • Preferences: Preferred job titles, locations, compensation ranges, and resume attachments.
  • OAuth Credentials: Google OAuth tokens (if you link your Gmail) to automate cold outreach and capture responses from recruiters.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to operate the IITIIMJobAssistant services:

  • Automating job searches, matching roles, and submitting job applications on your behalf.
  • Drafting and sending tailored cold emails to recruiters.
  • Maintaining your live activity feed and dashboard statistics.

3. Data Retention & Safety

We secure your data using industry-standard measures: AES-256 encryption for credentials and secure tokens, and SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit. We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. You can request complete deletion of your account and associated data from your dashboard settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our agent integrates with external platforms like Google (for email outreach) and LinkedIn (for profile management). We do not share your credentials directly with third parties. Payments are handled securely via Razorpay, and we do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

Security Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026
Quick Summary: We use industry-standard bank-grade encryption to secure your resumes, OAuth tokens, and account information. Your data is isolated and fully protected.

1. Data Encryption

All sensitive information is encrypted both in transit and at rest:

  • In Transit: All connections are protected using TLS 1.3 with strong cipher suites.
  • At Rest: Sensitive user data, resume databases, and credentials are encrypted using AES-256 GCM. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a high work factor.

2. Token Isolation & Revocability

Your Google and LinkedIn OAuth tokens are stored in an isolated credentials store. We request only the minimal scopes required to perform actions (sending/reading emails for cold outreach). You can revoke our access at any time directly from your Google/LinkedIn settings.

3. AWS Infrastructure

Our applications run in secure Virtual Private Clouds (VPCs) hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Database access is restricted to application instances, with automated hourly backups and active DDoS protection through AWS Shield.

4. Access Controls & Audits

Internal access to user databases is strictly limited to authorized personnel and is monitored through audit logs. We run regular automated vulnerability scans and plan yearly external penetration testing to ensure maximum defense.
